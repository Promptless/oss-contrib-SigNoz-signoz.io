## Collapsible (`collapsible`)

Default state is **expanded**. Click the header to toggle.

### `collapsible` — starts expanded

<Admonition type="note" collapsible title="Collapsible note (starts open)">
  This note is collapsible. Click the header to collapse it. `inline code` and [a link](https://signoz.io/docs/) still work.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="tip" collapsible>
  A collapsible tip. The chevron rotates to indicate state.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="warning" collapsible>
  A collapsible warning with `inline code` and [a link](https://signoz.io/docs/).
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="danger" collapsible>
  A collapsible danger admonition.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="important" collapsible>
  A collapsible important admonition.
</Admonition>

### `collapsible defaultCollapsed` — starts collapsed

<Admonition type="info" collapsible defaultCollapsed title="Collapsed by default — click to expand">
  You expanded me! `inline code` and [a link](https://signoz.io/docs/).
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="warning" collapsible defaultCollapsed>
  This warning starts collapsed.
</Admonition>
